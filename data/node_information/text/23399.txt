   Corp Events is an events agency that service the B2B events industry.

   ensure that Corp Events respect your privacy and that we interact with
   our customers in line with current legislation and in accordance to
   your wishes, in agreement with the General Data Protection Regulation
   (GDPR) in Europe.

   If you have any concerns with how your personal data may be used,
   please contact us on by filling in the form at the bottom of this page.

   Our commitment with data collection:
     * We respect your privacy and your choices regarding your personal
       data,
     * We protect and secure our data storage and processing,
     * We allow you to choose which email communication you wish to
       receive and we allow you unsubscribe from all of our mailing lists
       at any given time,
     * We allow you to view your stored data and easily practice your
       right to delete data by filling in the form below,
     * We collect some of your data in order to offer you content that
       fits to your expectations,
     * We collaborate with trusted and reliable partners.

What data do we collect as part of your Big Data Paris registration?

   As part of your conference and expo registration, we collect your
   personal data for a maximum of 3 years since your last consent. The use
   of your data is crucial for us in order to organize an event that is of
   the highest relevancy and accuracy for you. Although this data
   processing is mandatory as part of your participation in our event, you
   can request to erase your data from our database at any time by
   contacting us by filling in the form at the bottom of this page.

   The data we collect when you register:
     * Personal data: first name, last name, email address, company, job
       title, industry, department, phone number …
     * Data related to the event: discount codes, channel of
       communication, sources of your website visit…
     * Registration to some of our partner’s newsletter, only if you check
       the specific boxes on the registration form
     * If you check the subscription box at the end of registration, you
       would agree to receive information form media partners. Thus, your
       email address would be shared with those partners.

   We may also collect this data through other communication channels than
   the registration form. on this website. Other channels may include your
   responses to event-related surveys or contact with our sales team. In
   these specific cases, your consent is also required.

The use of your data

   We collect your data when you register to our event in order to execute
   the organization of our event. Your data can also be used to deliver
   information to you. In addition, if you explicitly provide your
   consent, it can be used to send you information from our partners.
    1. Secure Data Storage

   Your data is collected, stored, and managed securely through our
   trusted service providers (OVH, Salesforce, Eventmaker). We always
   intent to secure and protect the entire processing of your personal
   data with care.
    2. Communication and information about the event

   We use your contact information to inform you about the current event,
   on which you are registered. We also send you information about
   upcoming editions of the event and/or about other relevant events
   organized by Corp Events. You may also other content that be of
   interest to you and relevant to your profile.

   You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time by clicking on
   the unsubscribe link at the bottom of each of our communications.
    3. Deletion of your personal data

   Your data is sorted carefully and deleted 3 years after the collection.
   You can, however, request a deletion of your data at any time by
   filling the form below.
    4. Transmission of your data to third parties, with your consent

   Your data may also be collected by third parties only with your
   consent. If you meet an exhibitor at one of our conferences, the
   exhibitor could scan your badge with your consent in order to retrieve
   your personal data. By attending a product workshop, your badge is also
   scanned in order to transmit your contact information to the speaker of
   the workshop. To avoid this transmission of data, you can refuse to
   scan your badge at any time.

   In these cases of data collection by our partners, Corp Events is no
   longer responsible for the processing of your data. However, we can put
   you in touch with the partner at your request. In this context, we
   always work with trusted and reliable partners.

The Data Collected on Our Website https://bigdataparis.com

   This event website uses cookies to:
     * Offer personalized content to each visitor,
     * Improve your browsing and navigation experience on our website,
     * Follow statistical data on our web pages (with Google Analytics)

   You can modify your choice regarding the use of cookies at any time by
   clicking on the small icon at the bottom right of this page.
    1. What is a cookie

   Cookies are small files containing a few lines of text. Websites create
   cookies in order to store and reuse data on your browser. This data
   mainly contains information about your browsing and is stored on your
   own device through the web browser that you use.
    2. Our use of cookies on this website

   Identification cookies:

   In order to offer you personalized content, we use identification
   cookies to give you access to downloadable content, such as our
   magazine page and/or our sponsorship packages. These cookies are set up
   by third-party applications: Salesforce and Pardot.

   Operating cookies:

   These cookies are used to allow the proper configuration of this
   website and give you access to the main features on the site. We use it
   to store your choice about cookies.

   Audience cookies:

   In order to improve our services on our event, we use cookies to be
   able to track the frequency of visitors of this website. We also
   collect your traffic sources when you register to the event. In order
   to track visitor statistics on our website, we use third-party
   applications: Google Analytics and Google tag Management.
    3. Your prior consent

   You can accept or decline cookie collection at any time on this
   website, by clicking on the bottom on each page of this website. You
   can chance your preferences at any time.

   By accepting our privacy policy, cookies will be collected on your
   current browser for a maximum of 13 months. If you wish to block
   cookies on this website, the main cookies will be immediately deleted
   and blocked, until you change this setting.
    4. Blocked cookies

   Cookies store data about your visit on a website. Some of this data
   might be used to identify a specific visitor. In order to protect your
   privacy, we let you choose to block these cookies easily on each page
   of this website.

   In case you choose to block cookies, the following cookies are blocked:
     * The analytics tracking cookies
     * The tracking of your actions on the website

   However, some cookies might still remain:
     * The sources of traffic (anonymous)
     * Your choice about cookies is stored in a cookie record

   Corp Events is an events agency that service the B2B events industry.
   When you register or sign up to be part of our event, we are collecting

   ensure that Corp Events respect your privacy and that we interact with
   our customers in line with current legislation and in accordance to
   your wishes, in agreement with the General Data Protection Regulation
   (GDPR) in Europe.

   If you have any concerns with how your personal data may be used,
   please contact us on by filling in the form at the bottom of this page.

   Our commitment with data collection:
     * We respect your privacy and your choices regarding your personal
       data,
     * We protect and secure our data storage and processing,
     * We allow you to choose which email communication you wish to
       receive and we allow you unsubscribe from all of our mailing lists
       at any given time,
     * We allow you to view your stored data and easily practice your
       right to delete data by filling in the form below,
     * We collect some of your data in order to offer you content that
       fits to your expectations,
     * We collaborate with trusted and reliable partners.

What data do we collect as part of your Big Data Paris registration?

   As part of your conference and expo registration, we collect your
   personal data for a maximum of 3 years since your last consent. The use
   of your data is crucial for us in order to organize an event that is of
   the highest relevancy and accuracy for you. Although this data
   processing is mandatory as part of your participation in our event, you
   can request to erase your data from our database at any time by
   contacting us by filling in the form at the bottom of this page.

   The data we collect when you register:
     * Personal data: first name, last name, email address, company, job
       title, industry, department, phone number …
     * Data related to the event: discount codes, channel of
       communication, sources of your website visit…
     * Registration to some of our partner’s newsletter, only if you check
       the specific boxes on the registration form
     * If you check the subscription box at the end of registration, you
       would agree to receive information form media partners. Thus, your
       email address would be shared with those partners.

   We may also collect this data through other communication channels than
   the registration form. on this website. Other channels may include your
   responses to event-related surveys or contact with our sales team. In
   these specific cases, your consent is also required.

The use of your data

   We collect your data when you register to our event in order to execute
   the organization of our event. Your data can also be used to deliver
   information to you. In addition, if you explicitly provide your
   consent, it can be used to send you information from our partners.
    1. Secure Data Storage

   Your data is collected, stored, and managed securely through our
   trusted service providers (OVH, Salesforce, Eventmaker). We always
   intent to secure and protect the entire processing of your personal
   data with care.
    2. Communication and information about the event

   We use your contact information to inform you about the current event,
   on which you are registered. We also send you information about
   upcoming editions of the event and/or about other relevant events
   organized by Corp Events. You may also other content that be of
   interest to you and relevant to your profile.

   You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time by clicking on
   the unsubscribe link at the bottom of each of our communications.
    3. Deletion of your personal data

   Your data is sorted carefully and deleted 3 years after the collection.
   You can, however, request a deletion of your data at any time by
   filling the form below.
    4. Transmission of your data to third parties, with your consent

   Your data may also be collected by third parties only with your
   consent. If you meet an exhibitor at one of our conferences, the
   exhibitor could scan your badge with your consent in order to retrieve
   your personal data. By attending a product workshop, your badge is also
   scanned in order to transmit your contact information to the speaker of
   the workshop. To avoid this transmission of data, you can refuse to
   scan your badge at any time.

   In these cases of data collection by our partners, Corp Events is no
   longer responsible for the processing of your data. However, we can put
   you in touch with the partner at your request. In this context, we
   always work with trusted and reliable partners.

The Data Collected on Our Website https://bigdataparis.com

   This event website uses cookies to:
     * Offer personalized content to each visitor,
     * Improve your browsing and navigation experience on our website,
     * Follow statistical data on our web pages (with Google Analytics)

   You can modify your choice regarding the use of cookies at any time by
   clicking on the small icon at the bottom right of this page.
    1. What is a cookie

   Cookies are small files containing a few lines of text. Websites create
   cookies in order to store and reuse data on your browser. This data
   mainly contains information about your browsing and is stored on your
   own device through the web browser that you use.
    2. Our use of cookies on this website

   Identification cookies:

   In order to offer you personalized content, we use identification
   cookies to give you access to downloadable content, such as our
   magazine page and/or our sponsorship packages. These cookies are set up
   by third-party applications: Salesforce and Pardot.

   Operating cookies:

   These cookies are used to allow the proper configuration of this
   website and give you access to the main features on the site. We use it
   to store your choice about cookies.

   Audience cookies:

   In order to improve our services on our event, we use cookies to be
   able to track the frequency of visitors of this website. We also
   collect your traffic sources when you register to the event. In order
   to track visitor statistics on our website, we use third-party
   applications: Google Analytics and Google tag Management.
    3. Your prior consent

   You can accept or decline cookie collection at any time on this
   website, by clicking on the bottom on each page of this website. You
   can chance your preferences at any time.

   By accepting our privacy policy, cookies will be collected on your
   current browser for a maximum of 13 months. If you wish to block
   cookies on this website, the main cookies will be immediately deleted
   and blocked, until you change this setting.
    4. Blocked cookies

   Cookies store data about your visit on a website. Some of this data
   might be used to identify a specific visitor. In order to protect your
   privacy, we let you choose to block these cookies easily on each page
   of this website.

   In case you choose to block cookies, the following cookies are blocked:
     * The analytics tracking cookies
     * The tracking of your actions on the website

   However, some cookies might still remain:
     * The sources of traffic (anonymous)
     * Your choice about cookies is stored in a cookie record
