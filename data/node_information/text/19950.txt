   INFOS

   Perfumes are surely the most delicate
   and evanescent of all human creations.
   Yet they make a lasting impact on the memory.

The world’s only fragrance archive

   The idea behind the Osmothèque was to create a unique place devoted to
   preserving the memory of scents : a place that could inventory and
   round up existing perfumes and perfumes that would appear in the
   future, and that could trace certain great perfumes that no longer
   existed and bring them back to life.
