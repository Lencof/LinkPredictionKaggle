Who are we?

   Insee offers a service to disseminate information on the Sirene®
   registry and provides user support. The sirene.fr website presents
   these services based on the Sirene® database. Sirene.fr presents the
   data of the registry, and tools for creating lists or searching.

   This unique and exhaustive database of enterprises and their
   establishments receives over 10 000 daily updates and covers all
   sectors of activities. It contains economic and legal information on 28
   million establishments, of which 11 million are active.

Open Data dissemination

   There are 4 ways to access the Sirene® database:
    1. Download the Sirene database free of charge Pre-created files are
       available for free download on data.gouv.fr. The available files,
       covering all of France, are the following:
          + The complete list of enterprises (all active and ceased
            enterprises with regard to the their current state in the
            registery)
          + The complete record of history variables of enterprises (for
            each enterprise, the whole set of values of certain history
            variables in the Sirene registry)
          + The complete list of establishments (all active and closed
            establishments with regard to their current state in the
            registry)
          + The complete record of history variables of establishment (for
            each establishment, the whole set of values of certain history
            variables in the Sirene registry)
          + The complete record of establishments''s succession links
    2. Create a customised list of establishments free of charge You can
       obtain a list of establishment based on certain criteria that you
       select yourself (criteria of location, activity, legal form,
       workforce size classes, date of creation or date of the last
       update). Each list can include up to 200 000 establishments.
    3. Search for an enterprise Individual searches, based on name,
       surname, corporate names, Siren or Siret identifiers give the
       possibility to find an enterprise with all its establishments.
    4. API access An API based on Sirene database is available on INSEE
       API Catalogue.

Download the Sirene database free of charge

   Under the loi pour une République numérique Sirene data have been
   available as Open Data since the beginning of January 2017.
   You can download from data-gouv five monthly zipped files :
     * The complete list of enterprises (all active and ceased enterprises
       with regard to the their current state in the registery)
     * The complete record of history variables of enterprises (for each
       enterprise, the whole set of values of certain history variables in
       the Sirene registry)
     * The complete list of establishments (all active and closed
       establishments with regard to their current state in the registry)
     * The complete record of history variables of establishment (for each
       establishment, the whole set of values of certain history variables
       in the Sirene registry)
     * The complete record of establishments''s succession links

   In the Documentation section you will find a complete description of
   these files and their content.
