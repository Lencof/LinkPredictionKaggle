The Sports Centre of the University of Bologna

   CUSB, the Sports Centre of the University of Bologna, promotes amateur
   and competitive sports and athletics on the 5 campuses of the Alma
   Mater (Bologna, Forl√¨, Cesena, Ravenna and Rimini), offering over 150
   courses and 100 different sports and activities.
   CUSB has 6 sports centres and 30 different pitches; it also promotes
   and organizes 9 annual championships and 18 national and international
   tournaments. In the 2018/19 sports season, CUSB athletes won 8 gold, 7
   silver and 4 bronze medals. In particular, in 2019 the men‚Äôs basketball
   team won the European Universities Games for the third consecutive
   time, while the women‚Äôs volley team won the European Ultimate Frisbee
   Championship Finals for the first time.
